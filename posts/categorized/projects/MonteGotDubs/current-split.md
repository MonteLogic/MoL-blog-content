# Weekly Training Split

## Monday
**Theme:** Volume & Capacity (The Hardest Day)  
**Location:** Hills (AM) / Gym (PM)

### Morning: Sprint Capacity
- **Warm-up:** Dynamic stretching + A-skips
- **Acceleration:** 4 x 40m Sprints (Unresisted, Max Effort)
- **Intensive Tempo:** 3 x 200m @ 85% effort
  - Rest: 3 minutes between reps
- **Goal:** Deplete the tank

### Afternoon: The "Sleeves & Belt" Session
**Warm-up:**
1. Standing Oblique Push Out
2. Band Distracted Split Squats

**Primer:**
- Medium RDLs (Dumbbell): 2 x 10
  - *Note: Not heavy. Just to pump blood into hamstrings/lower back.*

**Main Event:**
- Banded Hack Squat: 3 x 8 reps
  - *Gear: Belt + Knee Sleeves*
  - *Intensity: Max. Fight the bands on the way up.*

**Accessory:**
- Keiser Leg Press Calf Raises: 3 x 12
- Keiser Back Extension Machine (1s hold at top)

---



## Wednesday
**Theme:** Speed Endurance (The "Dark Place")  
**Location:** Willy's Blvd

### Morning: The Broken 400
- **Warm-up:** 2 x 20m (Unresisted build-ups)

**The Set:**
1. Run 300m at 400m race pace
2. Rest exactly 60 seconds
3. Run 100m ALL OUT

- **Goal:** Lactic threshold pain tolerance

### Afternoon (Gym): The "Armor" Session
**Goal:** Blood flow and tendon health. Do not go to failure; chase the pump.

1. Keiser Leg Press Calf Raises: 3 x 12
   - *Note: Higher reps here to flush the Achilles after the morning sprint.*
2. Keiser Chest Press: 3 x 12
3. Keiser Back Extension: 3 x 8
   - *Note: Focus on the squeeze. This prepares your lower back for Friday.*
4. Keiser Hamstring Curls: 3 x 8

---

## Friday
**Theme:** Acceleration & Force (The Power Day)  
**Location:** Hills (AM) / Gym (PM)

### Morning: Acceleration Primers
- **Warm-up:** Dynamic stretching
- **The Work:** 6 x 20m Starts (Max Explosive Effort)
  - Rest: Full recovery (2-3 mins)
  - *Note: Do not rush. This is about quality and explosiveness, not fatigue.*

### Afternoon: The "Sleeves & Belt" Session
**Warm-up:**
1. Standing Oblique Push Out
2. Band Distracted Split Squats

**Primer:**
- Medium RDLs (Dumbbell): 3 x 8
  - *Note: Slightly more volume than Monday, but still sub-maximal.*

**Main Event:**
- Banded Hack Squat: 3 x 8 reps
  - *Gear: Belt + Knee Sleeves*
  - *Intensity: Max. Explode out of the hole.*

**Accessory:**
- Keiser Leg Press Calf Raises: 3 x 15 (Start here as requested)
- Keiser Back Extension Machine (1s hold at top)
